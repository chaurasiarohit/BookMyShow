ZOMBIELAND 2:DOUBLE TAP

SYNOPSIS

Through comic mayhem that stretches from the White House and through the heartland, the Zombie slayers face off against many new kinds of zombies that have evolved since the first part/installment of the movie, new human survivors and the growing pains of their own snarky, makeshift family. Zombieland 2, the sequel of Zombieland is post-apocalyptic zombie comedy film starring Woody Harrelson, Jesse Eisenberg, Emma Stone, Abigail Breslin and Bill Murray in prominent role.
